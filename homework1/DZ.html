The Simpsons

Bart simpson

Bartholomew JoJo "Bart Simpson" is one of the main characters in the animated series The Simpsons. Bart is the eldest child of Homer and Marge Simpson. He also has two younger sisters, Lisa and Maggie. Bart is the embodiment of the image of a brawler and a mediocre student at school. Along with his father, Bart is one of the most famous characters in this series.

Lisa Simpson

Lisa Mary Simpson is the daughter of Homer and Marge Simpson, an extremely intelligent, underdeveloped, socially active girl. He loves jazz and saxophone, is interested in philosophy, science, history. Environmental and animal rights activist. She became a Buddhist and a vegetarian.

Homer Simpson

Father of the family, smooth, bald, lazy and not very smart. He often behaves absurdly, selfishly and tactlessly, however, he remains nice.

March Simpson

Homer's wife, maiden name - Bouvier. A housewife, she spends most of her time at home caring for Maggie's youngest child, as well as Bart and Lisa, and even her husband. Her most famous feature is blue hair gathered in a high hairstyle.
